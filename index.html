About TPACK

TPACK (Technological Pedagogical Content Knowledge) describes the blend of knowledge teachers need to teach effectively with technology. It combines Content Knowledge (CK)—mastery of the subject, Pedagogical Knowledge (PK)—how to teach and assess, and Technological Knowledge (TK)—how to choose and use digital tools. When CK, PK, and TK overlap, teachers can design engaging, equitable, and technology-enhanced learning that improves outcomes.

CK

Depth in the subject area

PK

Strategies, assessment, classroom management

TK

Selecting tools that align with objectives
